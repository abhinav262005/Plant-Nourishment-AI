Overview
This project utilizes artificial intelligence to optimize plant nourishment through data-driven insights, automation, and precision agriculture techniques. By leveraging AI, growers can enhance plant health, improve yield, and ensure efficient resource utilization.
Features
- Automated Monitoring: AI-powered sensors and IoT devices continuously track soil moisture, nutrient levels, and environmental conditions.
- Predictive Analysis: Machine learning models predict plant health trends and recommend optimal nourishment strategies.
- Smart Irrigation: AI-driven irrigation systems adjust water supply based on plant needs, minimizing waste.
- Nutrient Management: AI analyzes soil composition to provide customized fertilization plans.
- Disease Detection: Computer vision detects early signs of diseases and suggests treatment options.
- Growth Optimization: AI models recommend ideal planting schedules and environmental conditions for maximum growth.
